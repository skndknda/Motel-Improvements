OUR BRANDS
 
On ChoiceHotels.com, you can book hotels for your business trip or meeting, find a leisure hotel for your weekend getaway or family vacation, or even a welcomed stop along the road. Our hotel brands range from Cambria® hotels & suites, specifically designed for a modern, VIP experience; to our iconic Comfort Inn® and Comfort Suites® hotels that provide the stay experience you need to be ready for anything while you’re away. Sleep Inn® hotels offer a simply stylish experience that is uplifting by design, while, Clarion® hotels are ideal business hotels, with meeting and event facilities that make it possible to get together for life’s critical moments.
 
Quality Inn®, Econo Lodge® and Rodeway Inn hotels offer hotel deals that deliver the real value you’re looking for when you travel. You’ll also find extended stay hotels like MainStay Suites® and Suburban Extended Stay Hotel® brand hotels that help you feel more at home while you are away for longer stays. Finally, let the destination reach you at the Ascend Hotel Collection® member hotels, one-of-a-kind, upscale hotels committed to connecting you with the local flavor of the area you are visiting.
 
With 10 different brands and the Ascend Hotel Collection membership program, we’re confident there’s at least one that is just what you’re looking for. Learn more about the brands in our portfolio to find the perfect fit your next trip.
